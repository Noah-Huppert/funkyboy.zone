Make UMass an anti-racism campus

Image credit: UMass Racial Justice Coalition . Petition for UMass students
Petition for non UMass students

This petition from the UMass Racial Justice Coalition is asking UMass to commit to becoming an anti racism institution. The petition asks for better responses to acts of hate, academic equity, racism prevention measures, and divestment from the UMass Police Department.
